Julia McGuire Wiki, Winter House, Boyfriend, Age, Family, Net Worth

May 2024 · 4 minute read

The mash-up of Summer House and Southern Charm, Bravo’s new series Winter House brought together cast members from both shows. But they also introduced some fresh faces including our star for that article Julia McGuire.

Keep reading this Julia McGuire Wiki to learn more about her.

Julia McGuire On Winter House

Bravo described Julia McGuire as a “blonde bombshell looking forward to a winter getaway with old friends and new.” And by “old friends”, they meant Julia’s childhood friend Paige DeSorbo from Southern Charm, and Winter House newbies Andrea Denver and Gabrielle Kniery.

Reportedly, Julia first met Paige at a modeling job while she was just 11, and the duo maintained a friendship ever since. As for the other two, they stumbled upon each other after running in the same modeling and social circles.

Like any other Bravo dating series, Winter House is bound to be filled with drunken squabbles, love triangles, tears, and hot tub hijinks. Unfortunately, Julia was in a committed relationship at the time. So, she might not be at the center of any of the romantic drama this season. However, she was hoping to gain some screentime and be a matchmaker for her friends.

Joining Julia as a newbie are Andrea Denver, Gabby Kniery, and Jason Cameron. 

As for the Bravo staples, they are Austen Kroll, Craig Conover, Kyle Cooke, Amanda Batula, Lindsay Hubbard, Paige DeSorbo, Luke Gulbranson, and Ciara Miller.

Promoting her appearance, Julia took it to her IG on October 20, 2021 (show’s premier day) to write, “One thing I love more than dogs is you tuning it to watch #WinterHouse on @bravotv at 9 PM TONIGHT!!”

Does Julia McGuire Have A Boyfriend?

Yes, Julia McGuire did have a boyfriend while entering Winter House in 2021. Though she liked to keep her mystery man as it is, he was spotted a few times over her socials. The last we saw him was on July 2021, as the couple pictured themselves in each other arms while vacationing in Milos, a volcanic Greek island in the Aegean Sea.

Also, over one of the pictures, Julia wrote, “dating a greek.” Hinting her boyfriend’s ethnicity.

Also, Julia’s boyfriend has been featured over her TikTok. Do check her TikTok post from October 2, 2021, captioned, “Say something about my water shoes… I dare you.”

Julia McGuire Age

Julia McGuire was born on November 23, 1992. That made her 29 years of age in 2021 when she first appeared on Winter House.

Corresponding to her birthday, Julia is of the Sagittarius zodiac.

Fun Fact: Julia shared her 2018 birthday felt a lot like the time she was dragged to a tea ceremony, hungover. Also, that was the year she lost her health insurance.

Julia McGuire Family

Julia McGuire is the daughter of Jennifer @jennifermcguire  and Jomar McGuire @jomar642. Her mother, Jennifer is a Brielle, NJ native who went to Manasquan High School. Whereas, Julia’s dad Jomar hails from  Kearny, NJ, and attended Kearny High School and Fairleigh Dickinson University.

Julia McGuire Net Worth

Julia McGuire garnered a net worth of under $300 thousand by 2021.

A model by profession, Julia has worked with the Wilhelmina Models for 7 years. She left the company in 2009, and a year later she was then recruited by APM Model Management for the next 2 years.

In 2021, she was represented by Select Model Management Miami and Abbey Lyn Models. Over her career, she has worked with brands like Maker’s Mark Bourbon and Hollie Watman swimwear. Also, she has graced the cover of Psychology Today and got featured in a Chico advertisement with her mother.

They say a model’s diet is ruthless, but Julia couldn’t care less. She is often seen over IG posing with a whole pizza, or just chowing down on BBQ. “Eat like no one’s watching and then post for everyone to see,” she said.
