Princess Sofia, Duchess of Vrmland

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Princess Sofia in 2015, photo by Hans_Chr/Bigstock.com

Birth Name: Sofia Kristina Hellqvist

Place of Birth: Danderyd Hospital, Danderyd, Sweden

Date of Birth: 6 December, 1984

Ethnicity: Swedish

Princess Sofia, Duchess of Värmland is the wife of Prince Carl Philip, Duke of Värmland.

She is the daughter of Marie Britt Rotman, a plastics industry marketing manager, and Erik Oscar Hellqvist, an employment counselor. Her father was born in Denmark, to Swedish parents. Her mother was born in Älvdalen, Dalama, Sweden. Princess Sofia has been a glamour model and reality television contestant. She has three children with Prince Carl.

Princess Sofia’s paternal grandfather was Stig Ingvar Hellqvist (the son of Erik Andreas Emanuel Hellqvist and Frida Kristina Fredriksson). Stig was born in Malmö, Hylte Limhamn, Skåne, Sweden. Erik was the son of Anders Fredrik Hellqvist and Anna Charlotta Eriksdotter. Frida was the daughter of Joel Fredrik Snygg and Johanna Magnusdotter.

Princess Sofia’s paternal grandmother was Ingrid Ann-Marie Nilsson (the daughter of Oskar Petrus Nilsson and Lilly Kristina Månsson). Oskar was the son of Karl Ferdinand Nilsson and Hedda Vilhelmina Jonsdotter. Lily was the daughter of Carl August Månsson and Emma Helena Persdotter.

Princess Sofia’s maternal grandfather was Janne Herbert Ribbe Rotman (the son of Lars Gotthard Rotman and Maria Elisabet Åhs). Janne was born in Älvdalen, Dalarna. Lars was the son of Jan Persson Rotman and Anna Persdotter. Maria was the daughter of Per Johan Åhs and Gum Anna Maria Persdotter.

Princess Sofia’s maternal grandmother is Britt Ingegerd Dahlberg (the daughter of Per Gustaf Dahlberg and Grund Anna Maria Johansson). Per was the son of Per Gabrielsson Dahlberg and Hilma Maria Hansdotter. Grund was the daughter of Grund Erik Johan Olsson and Knif Ida Maria Andersdotter.
