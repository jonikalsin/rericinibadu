Miles Teller Net Worth: How Much Is the Whiplash Star Worth in 2023?

September 2024 · 5 minute read

Miles Teller is one of the most talented and versatile actors of his generation. He has starred in a variety of films, from indie dramas to blockbuster franchises, and has earned critical acclaim and awards recognition for his performances. But how much is Miles Teller’s net worth in 2023? And what are the sources of his income and wealth? In this article, we will explore the career and finances of the Whiplash star, and reveal some interesting facts about his life and work.

Early Life and Education

Miles Teller was born on February 20, 1987 in Pennsylvania to parents Merry, a real estate agent, and Michael, a nuclear power plant engineer. He has two older sisters, Erin and Dana. His paternal grandfather was of Russian-Jewish descent, and his ancestry also includes English and Irish forebears.

Teller spent his early years in Pennsylvania and Delaware before his family moved to Citrus County, Florida, at age twelve. Growing up, he was involved with acting, was president of his high school’s drama club, and played alto saxophone, drums, piano and guitar. He also played baseball competitively and had dreams of turning professional.

In 2007, Teller was a passenger in a car that lost control at 80 mph (130 km/h) and flipped eight times. He has multiple scars on his face from the crash.

Teller went on to earn a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Tisch School of the Arts at New York University in 2009. He then studied method acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute and screen acting with Stonestreet Studios.

Acting Career and Breakthrough

Teller made his film debut in Rabbit Hole (2010), after Nicole Kidman handpicked him for the role. He then starred in the remake of Footloose (2011), the comedy Project X (2012), and the romantic drama The Spectacular Now (2013), opposite Shailene Woodley. He and Woodley won the Sundance Film Festival Special Jury Prize for Dramatic Acting for their work in The Spectacular Now.

Teller’s breakthrough role came in Whiplash (2014), where he played a drummer who tries to impress his abusive jazz teacher (J.K. Simmons). The film was a critical and commercial success, and earned Teller nominations for the Gotham Independent Film Award for Best Actor, the Satellite Award for Best Actor – Motion Picture and the BAFTA Rising Star Award.

Teller also starred in the Divergent film series (2014-2016), again with Woodley, as well as the superhero film Fantastic Four (2015) and the biographical film War Dogs (2016), alongside Jonah Hill.

In recent years, Teller has appeared in films such as Only the Brave (2017), Thank You for Your Service (2017), Bleed for This (2016), Top Gun: Maverick (2022), Spiderhead (2022) and The Offer (2022). He has also starred in the Amazon Prime Video crime drama Too Old to Die Young (2019).

Net Worth and Income Sources

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Miles Teller’s net worth is $16 million as of 2023. His main source of income is his acting career, which has earned him salaries ranging from $8,000 for Whiplash to $2 million for Fantastic Four.

Teller also has other sources of income, such as endorsements, investments and brand deals. He is a spokesperson for Men’s Wearhouse, a clothing retailer that sells suits and tuxedos. He also invested in Long Drink, a Finnish drink company that produces a canned alcoholic beverage made from gin and grapefruit soda.

Teller lives a comfortable lifestyle with his wife Keleigh Sperry, a model whom he married in 2019. The couple owns a $3.9 million home in Studio City, California, which features four bedrooms, five bathrooms, a pool, a spa and a fire pit.

  • Teller is a talented musician who can play the piano, guitar, saxophone and drums. He performed all of his drumming scenes in Whiplash without any doubles or editing.

  • Teller has a tattoo of a turtle on his left shoulder, which he got when he was 18. He said it represents his slow and steady approach to life.

Conclusion

Miles Teller is one of the most successful and respected actors of his generation. He has shown his versatility and talent in a wide range of films, from indie gems to blockbuster hits.
