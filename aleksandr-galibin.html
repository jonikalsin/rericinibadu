The director of My Little Sister, Aleksandr Galibin, is popular for his stunning acts in enormous television series and movies. Some of his recent masterpieces include Deadly Illusions, Black Sun, and The Road to Calvary. 

He was born on 27 September 1955 in Saint Petersburg in Russia. He is 5 ft and 10 inches (178 cm) tall. He has a pair of grey eyes with dark brown hair. 

Career Outset of Galibin

Ever before Galibin came on TV shows and movies, he used to be a theatre actor. He used to act at the Theatre of Youth Creativity. Later, in 1976, he made a debut in the movie I drugie ofitsialnye litsa. He portrayed Yura Ivanov in this movie.

Further, his film career was followed by many other movies and TV series like Traktir na Pyatnitskoy, Alye pogony, and more.

Is Galibin Married?

Galibin is a married Russian actor. He was married twice. In 1991, Aleksandr wedded Ruth Wyneken but things did not go right and they got separated in 1999.

Later, he took vows with his present wife Irina Savitskova. They have not made their marriage date public yet.

Where Did Aleksandr Study?

Galibin finished high school in St. Petersburg. later, he attended the Leningrad Institute of Theatre, Music, and Cinematography. Aleksandr got admitted in 1973 and later graduated in 1977 from the same university.

Additionally, Galibin went to Moscow where he studied film direction.

Awards & Achievements

Galibin has won two awards for his acts. In 2006, he became the winner of the People's Artist of the Russian Federation.
