Are Topps Now Cards a Good Investment in 2024?

May 2024 · 4 minute read

In recent years, the world of trading card collecting has witnessed a resurgence, attracting both enthusiasts and investors. Among the various trading card manufacturers,

Topps has emerged as a venerable name, celebrated for its iconic cards across an array of sports and entertainment franchises.

The Allure of Topps Now Cards

Topps, with its establishment in 1938, has maintained a dominant position in the trading card industry for decades.

The company has produced cards for sports like baseball, football, basketball, and hockey, along with non-sports entertainment franchises such as Star Wars, Pokémon, and Garbage Pail Kids. The allure of Topps Now cards lies in various factors:

  • Historical Significance: Vintage Topps cards capture historical moments and legendary athletes from bygone eras, thereby holding historical value and rarity that can lead to appreciable financial gains.

  • Nostalgia: Many collectors have a deep emotional connection to Topps cards, having grown up with them, which drives demand for specific cards and enhances their value.

  • Iconic Designs: Topps is known for its innovative and memorable card designs, contributing to their desirability among collectors and investors.

  • Player and Franchise Popularity: Cards featuring renowned athletes or beloved entertainment franchises can command significant prices, particularly when well-preserved and in high demand.

    • Investment Potential of Topps Now Cards

    While collecting Topps Now cards can be an enjoyable hobby, it's crucial to assess their investment potential. Several factors need consideration when determining whether Topps Now cards are a viable financial strategy:

  • Card Condition: The condition of a card is paramount, as mint or near-mint cards tend to fetch higher prices. Investing in protective sleeves and proper storage is essential to maintain card quality.

  • Scarcity and Rarity: The rarity of a card strongly influences its value. Low print runs, limited editions, and rare variations can substantially raise prices.

  • Market Trends: Staying informed about the trading card market is crucial. Trends evolve, and the popularity of players or franchises can fluctuate, impacting card values.

  • Authentication and Grading: Professional authentication and grading by organizations like PSA or Beckett can significantly enhance a card's value. Higher grades often lead to higher prices.

  • Long-Term vs. Short-Term: It's important to define the investment horizon, as some cards appreciate quickly, while others require patience over the long term to see significant returns.

  • Research and Expertise: Investing time in researching Topps Now cards of interest, engaging with online forums, attending card shows, and seeking advice from experienced collectors to make informed decisions is crucial.

    • Types of Sports Card Investing Strategies

    Understanding the various investment strategies related to sports card investing can provide valuable insights into the potential of Topps Now cards as an investment in 2024. Here are some different investment strategies based on the types of sports cards:

    1. Rookie Sports Card Investing

    Rookie card investing is a low-risk, high-reward strategy. It involves identifying players with the potential of becoming the next sports icons and investing in their rookie cards. While rookie cards are usually less expensive, if the players perform well, the card prices are likely to skyrocket.

    2. Vintage Sports Card Investing

    Investing in vintage sports cards can be a potentially attractive option for a portfolio. Unlike rookie cards, vintage cards tend to be expensive while offering the best returns. Notable vintage sports cards can include those of legendary players with historical significance.

    3. Graded Sports Card Investing

    The grading of a card is crucial in sports card investing, as it proves its authenticity and increases its price. Graded cards tend to increase in value more than ungraded ones, especially in unstable market conditions. It is advisable to opt for cards graded by reputed grading bodies such as PSA or Beckett.

    4. Milestone Sports Card Investing

    Milestone sports cards commemorate special moments in the career of a player or team. Investing in milestone cards before significant sporting events and predicting the performance of players can lead to substantial increases in the value of those cards.

    Topps Now Cards as an Investment in 2024

    Given the factors and investment strategies discussed, Topps Now cards hold significant potential as an investment in 2024.

    It is important to recognize that while not all cards appreciate in value, certain rare and highly sought-after Topps Now cards have demonstrated impressive returns over time. Although, it's essential to understand that trading card collecting and investing carry inherent risks, with no guarantee of profits.

    For those passionate about collecting Topps cards and willing to dedicate effort to research, preservation, and diversification, it can be a fulfilling pursuit both personally and financially. 

    They present an exciting opportunity for individuals looking to diversify their investment portfolios with tangible assets that hold historical and sentimental value. 

    Topps Now cards stand as a compelling investment option in 2024, combining historical significance, emotional value, and potential financial gains, making them an attractive choice for collectors and investors alike.
