UFC on ESPN: Nicolau vs. Perez Betting Guide

July 2024 · 2 minute read

The UFC on ESPN: Nicolau vs. Perez is an upcoming mixed martial arts event produced by the Ultimate Fighting Championship.

It's scheduled to take place on April 27, 2024, at the UFC Apex facility in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States.

The event will feature an exciting fight card with several compelling matchups across different weight classes.

Where to Watch

  • Main Card: The main card will start at 7:00 PM ET and can be watched on ESPN/ESPN+.
  • Prelims: The prelims will begin at 4:00 PM ET and will be available on ESPN2/ESPN+.

Participants

The event features a diverse lineup of fighters across various weight classes:

  • Flyweight - Main Event: Matheus Nicolau vs. Alex Perez
  • Light Heavyweight: Ryan Spann vs. Bogdan Guskov
  • Welterweight: Tim Means vs. Uroš Medić
  • Bantamweight: Rani Yahya vs. Victor Henry
  • Women's Strawweight: Ariane Lipski vs. Karine Silva
  • And more exciting matchups in different weight divisions
