Who Is Judd Trump?

Former world champion Judd Trump is a British professional snooker player who has won a career Triple down.

Judd was born on 20 August 1989 in Bristol. He was born to parents Georgina Trump and Steve Trump. In the family, he has one brother named Jack Trump.

At the age of 23, Judd Trump won the International Championship and became the number one player for the first time. In 2019, Judd lifted the Master trophy for the first time beating Ronnie O’Sullivan 10-4 in the finale.
