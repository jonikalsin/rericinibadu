Brooklyn McKnight Biography

Brooklyn McKnight is the daughter of Mindy and Shaun McKnight. On 31 December 1999, she and her twin Bailey McKnight were born. They will turn 23 in 2023. Additionally, her mother is a well-known YouTuber who regularly posts hairstyle tutorials, and her father is in charge of the technical aspects of the family’s YouTube channel.

Brooklyn, likewise, has a total of five siblings. Bailey, Rylan, and Kamri Noel are her three sisters. Additionally, she has two adopted siblings, Daxton and Paisley. Her siblings frequently make appearances in her YouTube videos. Additionally, Brooklyn is an American citizen who was born in Allen, Texas. Capricorn is her zodiac sign.

Regarding Brooklyn’s education, no mention is made of her previous institutions. However, the twins announced in April 2018 that they would be attending Baylor University in Waco, Texas, where they would major in Entrepreneurship.

Career

Brooklyn and Bailey, the twins, made their YouTube debut in 2009 in their mother’s video. They devote their videos to teen interest, fashion, beauty, and all things enjoyable.
Similarly, the twins launched another YouTube channel dedicated to “all things twins” in November 2015. The channel is called “Squared,” and they recently launched a web series of the same name. Seven sets of twins from North America, Australia, and the United Kingdom were featured in the videos.
Additionally, the McKnight twins are singers. They made their debut on 3 March 2017 with the single “Dance Like Me.” “SiMPLE THiNGS” and “What We’re Made Of” are the band’s other singles. They previously collaborated with Peter Hollins in August 2015 on a cover of Lennon and Maisy’s song “A Life That’s Good Can Be Bad.” Additionally, in 2017, the twins launched their own merchandise line.
