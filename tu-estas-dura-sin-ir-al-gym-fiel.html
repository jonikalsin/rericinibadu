Overall Meaning

In Marito's song "Tu Estas Dura Sin Ir al Gym", he sings about being infatuated with a woman he desires. The first few lines talk about how fast time is passing and how he has her now, but tomorrow she may be gone. He questions what they are doing and mentions that they are both in too deep, and there is no turning back. He compliments her and mentions how he is faithful to her, even though they are not together. He lets her make the decisions because he is willing to go along with whatever she wants.


Marito mentions that the woman he desires is physically fit and wears expensive designer clothes and lingerie. He also hints that she has a tough exterior and is not easily swayed by emotions as she puts an end to all her relationships. He tells her how he feels and how he wants to spend the night with her. He is the impulsive one while she is the more rational one. He describes her as someone who has left everything behind, but nobody leaves her behind.


The song portrays the desire and lust for someone and the willingness to go with the flow regardless of what the future holds. It talks about the physical attraction and how it can overpower rational thoughts and common sense.
