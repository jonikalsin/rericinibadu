Vladimir Petkovic’s Bio

Vladimir is a former Swiss football player and manager. He was born in Sarajevo, Bosnia, and Herzegovina, and served as the national team coach of Switzerland from 2014 to 2021. Vladimir started his football career when he was 15 years old. He joined the football team FK Sarajevo, and it was via this club that he began his professional career. He moved to Switzerland from Yugoslavia in search of a better future.

He played football for 20 years as a midfielder in Switzerland, Bosnia, and Slovenia. He spent most of his time in Switzerland, as well as Bellinzona, Young Boys, and Sion. After retiring from football, he spent two years as manager of the Italian club Lazio. In 2013, he guided his club to victory in the Coppa Italia. He was appointed head coach of Switzerland’s national team in 2014. He represented Switzerland at the UEFA EURO 2016 in France and the FIFA World Cup 2018 in Russia. He also guided the Swiss squad to Euro 2020, where they were drawn into Group A against Italy, Wales, and Turkey. Since being appointed as head coach, the Swiss have had great success.
