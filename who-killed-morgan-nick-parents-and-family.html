Everybody is wondering to know that who killed Morgan Nick. Here is everything about Morgan Nick’s Disappearance.

Morgan Nick, 6, disappeared from a minor league baseball game in Alma, Arkansas, on June 9, 1995, at 10:45 pm by an unidentified male.

While watching the game with her mother, she joined some friends in catching lightning bugs.

After stopping to clear the sand from her shoes, Morgan was last seen standing near her mother’s car.

Morgan was about 4 feet tall and 55 pounds at the time of her disappearance, with blonde hair and blue eyes. Her last known outfit consisted of a green Girl Scout t-shirt, blue denim shorts, and white tennis shoes.
