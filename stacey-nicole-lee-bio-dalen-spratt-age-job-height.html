Meet Dalen Spratt's Wife, Stacey Nicole Lee! Her Bio, Age, Job, Height

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Congratulations to Stacey Nicole Lee and her husband Dalen Spratt on their first baby named Axel Lee Spratt. The two welcomed their firstborn on November 2021, just seven months after their wedding.

Below this “Stacey Nicole Lee Bio” is everything you need to know about this new mother. 

Meet Stacey Nicole Lee, Dalen Spratt’s Wife

Stacey Nicole Lee first met the Ghost Brothers: Lights Out star Dalen Spratt back in 2007, while obtaining their MBAs at Clark Atlanta University. The two instantly fell in love with each other and knew he/she was “the one.”

According to Stacey, “I knew I found the one in Dalen because I didn’t just love him, I wasn’t just attracted to him, I liked him as a person.”  Although being lovebirds, the duo would laugh and talk as friends, and share about everything.  “I love his love for his family and especially how he treats and loves on his mother. He is my homey, lover, and friend,” she added.

As for Dalen, he first knew she was “the one” when he took a job delivering groceries to make some extra cash after her television show wasn’t renewed at the time. “She never once made me feel any type of way and thoroughly supported my decision. Any time I would feel down she would crack jokes about it to make me smile,” he shared.

Knowing that Stacey would stick with him at his lowest — “She deserved to be with me at my highest,” Dalen remarked.

Years of enjoying the dating bliss, Dalen finally dared to pop the question in 2021. He took Stacey on a trip to Cabo and proposed to her on a beach outside their hotel with rose petals in the shape of a huge heart stating “Will you marry me.”

One Stacey said “yes”, the fireworks lit up the sky.

“I was in shock (and) had no idea the proposal was about to take place. I don’t even remember saying yes I just remember screaming and crying and shaking my head,” Stacey recalled.

Stacey Nicole Lee Wedding, Kids

It was on April 21, 2021, Stacey and Dalen held their wedding at Aston Gardens in Atlanta, GA. The ceremony was attended by 9 guests including Dalen’s mother officiating and his Ghost Brothers by his side.

Also, their intimate celebration was a safe one, as guests were tested before the wedding day and 75 percent of them were already fully COVID vaccinated.

As for their “official” honeymoon, the couple was all set to take a trip to Bora Bora. 

For those of you who want to learn more, their wedding was featured in an episode of TLC’s Say Yes to the Dress. Do check it out.

Talking about Stacey’s kid, she welcomed her first child named Axel Lee Spratt, on September 14, 2021. Her daughter’s name comes from the Showtime drama Billions. Stacey explained that they fell in love with the main character Bobby Axelrod from the show, and wanted her child to be “intelligent, and strong, and bold” as the character. So, they ended up taking out the “Axel “part and loving its meaning — My Father is Peace, they decided on the name.

Stacey Nicole Lee Age

Stacey Nicole was reportedly born on May 5, 1977. That made her 44 years of age in 2021; nine years older than Dalen.

Corresponding to her birthday, Stacey is of the Taurus zodiac.

Stacey Nicole Lee Job

Stacey Nicole Lee has worked as the director of events at Haute Behavior (a marketing firm based in Atlanta, GA) for over 15 years. Over her time, she has confessedly managed over a $1.8 million budget, delivered and planned 100% of projects under budget, managed programming with an annual budget of $2.5 million, and executed Coca-Cola’s “Sprite Step off Challenge” preliminary competitions.

Before her job at Haute, Stacey worked as a corporate staff auditor of General Motors from Jan 2002 to April 2006.

Also, during her time at General Motors, Stacey was training herself for beauty pageants. On September 27, 2003, she won “Miss Michigan USA” in Port Huron.
