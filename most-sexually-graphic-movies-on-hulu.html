‘All About E,’ directed by Louise Wadley, is a romantic comedy film about E, a young Arabic-Australian DJ whose life takes an unexpected turn when she discovers a bag full of cash and decides to retain it. The film ‘Beach Rats’ depicts how social pressure and self-denial can lead to a person’s demise. Frankie, the main character in the film, has a difficult life. ‘Disobedience,’ a romantic drama film based on Naomi Alderman’s novel of the same name, examines themes of sexuality and faith. The film follows Ronit Krushka, a New York-based photographer who, following the death of her father, returns to London’s ultra-orthodox Jewish community.

Ma Ma (2015)


Penelope Cruz’s status as one of the most prominent actors of her generation has long been established because to her collaborations with Pedro Almodovar. This picture sees her collaborating with Spanish filmmaker Julio Medem, and we can simply state that it features one of the actress’s best performances. Cruz plays Magda, a single mother who works as a teacher and lives a relatively calm existence in this film. Magda’s life is turned upside down when she learns she has breast cancer.

Magda meets two men during her treatment who show her that love and life can still have value even when everything else seems to be lost. One of these guys is her son’s football coach, while the other is the doctor to whom she goes for therapy. The film could have done honor to Cruz’s great performance as the primary character if it had a better screenplay with more detailed characters.
